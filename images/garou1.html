One Punch Man Chapter 84

Doffy 12/08/17
52
0

No, I’m not going to put some spoiler warning or any of that. I don’t care. I don’t. This is how you write a chapter of manga. It was a fucking rollercoaster of hype and emotion. One insanely good page after another. The centipede, Garou, Blast, Genoa, And even Saitama(surprisingly). I came out of this chapter understanding Genos and Garou more, so unbelievably satisfied, and unbearably craving more. Amazing.
